About Me

I grew up in the suburbs of New Jersey and received my B.S. in Biology (Concentration: Microbiology, Minors: Fungal Biology & Infectious Disease) from Cornell University in Ithaca, NY. I decided to stay at Cornell to pursue my Ph.D. in Microbiology, where I am currently finishing my second year and preparing for my A-exam. In my free time, I enjoy cooking, gardening, and wandering around the woods with my dog, Kai, in search of cool bugs and mushrooms!
